Gift Baskets

For any occasion, perfect for corporate and personal giving, the delivery of a gourmet basket from Truffles and Trifles is the extra personal touch that makes a difference. Whether for an important client, a friend, a teacher, a neighbor, a secretary, your wife, or your husband, your basket carries with it an important message—that it is a gift you have chosen with great care. Each basket is customized to your needs and is elegantly prepared with the world’s most unique items…hundreds of treasures from which to choose! All wrapped in fine imported papers and tied in grand bows, your basket can be delivered or shipped by Truffles and Trifles to any location in the world.
